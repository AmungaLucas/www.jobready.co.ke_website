About Safaricom PLC

Safaricom PLC is Kenya's largest telecommunications operator, serving over 40 million subscribers across the country. Founded in 1999 and listed on the Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE: SCOM), Safaricom has transformed Kenya's digital landscape through innovative products and services that connect people, businesses, and communities.

Best known for M-PESA, the world's most successful mobile money platform, Safaricom processes over 50 million transactions daily. M-PESA has become a cornerstone of Kenya's economy, enabling financial inclusion for millions of previously unbanked citizens and inspiring similar services across Africa and beyond.

Beyond mobile money, Safaricom operates Kenya's most extensive 4G and 5G network, provides enterprise cloud and cybersecurity solutions, and invests heavily in digital innovation through its Safaricom Alpha and Spark programs. The company is committed to sustainable business practices and community development through the Safaricom Foundation.
