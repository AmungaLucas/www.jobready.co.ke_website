FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our CV writing services.

How long does it take to write my CV?
Standard delivery is 24 hours. Premium tier gets 6-hour delivery. Professional tier is delivered within 12 hours.
What information do you need from me?
Your current CV (if any), target job description, and a brief WhatsApp chat about your experience. We keep it simple — no long forms.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer free revisions. If you're still not satisfied, we'll work with you until it's perfect. Your satisfaction is our priority.
Can you write a CV for a specific job application?
Absolutely! Share the job description and we'll tailor your CV to match. Our keyword optimization ensures you pass ATS screening for that specific role.
What payment methods do you accept?
M-Pesa (Paybill/Till), bank transfer, and Airtel Money. Payment details are shared via WhatsApp once your order is confirmed.
Do you help with government job applications?
Yes! We specialize in Kenyan government CV format, including the required declaration sections, referee details, and the specific layout expected by public sector recruiters.
