Senior Software Engineer

Job Description

Join Safaricom's Technology division as a Senior Software Engineer. You'll lead the architecture and delivery of mission-critical platforms powering M-PESA, voice services, and enterprise solutions that serve over 40 million Kenyans daily. This is a high-visibility role reporting to the Head of Engineering with direct impact on products used by millions.

What You'll Do

  • Architect and build scalable microservices in Java, Python, and Node.js handling 50M+ daily transactions
  • Lead technical design reviews, establish coding standards, and mentor engineers across 4 product squads
  • Design and operate cloud-native infrastructure on AWS and Azure with 99.99% uptime targets
  • Drive performance optimisation for low-latency real-time payment processing systems
  • Collaborate with product, security, and data teams to ship customer-facing features end-to-end
  • Champion CI/CD best practices, automated testing, and infrastructure-as-code

What We're Looking For

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or equivalent (Master's preferred)
  • 5+ years building production-grade backend systems at scale
  • Deep expertise in Java, Python, or Node.js — ideally polyglot
  • Hands-on with containerisation (Docker, Kubernetes) and cloud platforms (AWS/Azure/GCP)
  • Strong grasp of distributed systems, event-driven architecture, and database design (MySQL, PostgreSQL, Redis)
  • Experience leading teams or mentoring — this is a senior individual contributor track with leadership scope

Compensation & Benefits

  • Competitive base salary of KSh 200,000 – 350,000 per month (negotiable based on experience)
  • Annual performance bonus up to 3 months' salary
  • Comprehensive medical, dental, and optical cover for employee + dependants
  • Hybrid work arrangement — 3 days in Westlands office, 2 days remote
  • KSh 100,000 annual professional development budget (courses, conferences, certifications)
  • Access to on-site gym, cafeteria, and wellness programmes

About Safaricom PLC

Safaricom is East Africa's leading telecommunications provider, connecting over 40 million subscribers through mobile voice, data, and financial services. Our M-PESA platform processes billions of dollars annually and has become a global case study in mobile money innovation. We're committed to transforming lives through technology and are investing heavily in cloud, AI, and digital platforms.
